Oklahoma Earthquake History

  • Oklahoma's earthquake history is complex and under researched.

  • The Meers Fault in SW Oklahoma has the potential to be one of the most dangerous in the United States.

  • Earthquakes in this region are felt over very large areas. Even as far North as Chicago.
